QUe

QUe is an open source conference app solution for Android and iOS.

QUe for Android

  • Objective-C
  • Swift

QUe has been used for the following scientific conferences:
  • Interspeech 2013 (Android || iOS )
  • Interspeech 2014 (Android || iOS)
  • Interspeech 2015 (Android || iOS)
  • DAGA 2014 (Android || iOS)
  • DAGA 2015 (Android || iOS)
  • DAGA 2016 (Android || iOS)
  • DGA 2014 (Android || iOS)
